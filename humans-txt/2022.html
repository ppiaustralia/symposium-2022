/* OBJECTIVE AND PURPOSE /*
‍
This Project Started on November 2021 ( Research Phase ) and the Website Created on February 2022 and are separated into three milestones
- To minimize annual costs and to create a website that is maintainable yet contemporary in the long term,
- To provide re-usable components and materials for future project leads, designers, and editors,
- To implement a No-Code System; Hence making sure the following people that overseas this project is not required to get a Developer of a certain programming language to either maintain or edit the website content or design,
- To implement a REUSABLE website with tutorials (that prioritizes on Usability Engineering and Design Thinking Methodology) that is teachable to either Designer, Developer, Digital Marketers, In-Between, or those who are not within these areas
- To seperate pages into multiple sub-domains located in PPIA server
